# Re-imagining the Resident App

When your residents log into their portal, they aren't looking for a management tool—they are looking for a service layer over their home.

Today, we're proud to announce **Resident Experience 2.0**, a complete overhaul of the Nest n Wings resident-facing application, built from the ground up for speed, transparency, and delight.

## What's New?

### 1. 1-Click Payments (Apple Pay & UPI)

Rent collection should be invisible. Residents can now pay their upcoming rent, clear late fee balances, or purchase add-on services entirely via Apple Pay, Google Pay, or UPI. 

No more switching tabs, entering card numbers manually, or sending screenshots to the admin via WhatsApp.

### 2. Maintenance with Transparency

Nothing frustrates a resident more than a broken AC unit and radio silence. 

The new maintenance ticketing system introduces real-time tracking. Residents can snap a photo, upload it, and watch the status change from *Received* -> *Assigned to Maintenance* -> *In Progress* -> *Resolved*.

They even get a push notification when the technician is outside the door.

### 3. Community Board (Beta)

Co-living isn't just about the bed; it's about the people. 

We've introduced a minimalist community board where residents can orchestrate weekend trips, sell used monitors, or find a gym buddy. It's fully moderated by your community managers but deeply empowering for your residents.

Welcome to the future of home.
